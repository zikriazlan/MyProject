ABOUT

AMIR VISION SDN. BHD. (AVSB) is a 100% bumiputera company that was established in April 2012. Started operating in Pulau Kambing, this company has been directly involved in the field of supply food and equipment to the ‘Oil and Gas’ industry for more than 7 years. In 2020, AVSB has gone a step further by venturing into the food processing industry by focusing on fish products and seafood. Accordingly, AVSB has moved to Chendering Industrial Area, Kuala Terengganu on premises equipped with machines, facilities and equipment to produce frozen products such as frozen minced, fish(otoshimi) and frozen keropok lekor under 'AV Seafood' brand.
